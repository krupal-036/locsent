How to Use LocSent

For Administrators

  • Live Map: The main map shows the last known location of all registered users. Markers update automatically every 30 seconds.
  • Geofences: To add or edit a geofence, simply manage the rows in your `Geofences` database in Notion. The map will reflect these changes.
  • Control Sign-Up: In the "Admin Controls" panel, you can enable or disable new user registration at any time.
  • Manage Users: In the "User Management" table:
    • Click History to view a user's last 10 location logs.
    • Click Export to download a user's complete history as a CSV, HTML, or PDF file.
    • Click Delete to permanently remove a user and all their associated data.

For Users

  • Sign Up & Login: If enabled by the admin, create an account to access your personal dashboard.
  • Your Personal Map: Your dashboard features a map showing your current location and all the admin-defined geofence zones. Your marker will update periodically.
  • Send Location Manually: Click the "Send Current Location" button to immediately log your coordinates and send them to the admin.
  • Automatic Tracking: While you are logged in, your location is also sent automatically in the background every hour.
